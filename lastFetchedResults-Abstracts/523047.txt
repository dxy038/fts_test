The main objective of this research is to study the duration and quality of spending leisure times by official staff in branches of Islamic Azad University (region 8). Using a survey and administering a questionnaire to 340 subjects the necessary data were gathered. The variables were gender, age, marriage status, income, education level, and university degree. The results showed that relation between gender, marriage status, and income level with quality of spending leisure times are significant (p<0.05 and 0.01). Also, it showed that "to be with family" was more current method of spending leisure times.
