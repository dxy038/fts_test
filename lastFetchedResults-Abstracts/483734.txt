Public advertisement or crime prevention advertisement is one of the
police tools for enhancement of preventive disciplinary measures and is
used by various types in different countries. According to the several studies
pure implementation of preventive disciplinary measures taken by police has
a limited effect on prevention, if police implements public advertisement
along with preventive measures to transfer the society, the implication of
preventive measures of police has intensified and covers wide range of
people. For the purpose of effective preventive advertisement, it is
necessary to define the audiences well. In addition, at the time of designing
it, time and location of seeing the advertisement by the audiences is taken
into account. The targeting community must be well defined and message
must distribute in the small geographical district and target the certain crime
as each of the audiences, by seeing considers themselves as the objective
example of it.
