The Internet represents a technology that will in the near future drastically change the structures of the existing production-, logistics- and service networks. These structures will be &#8220;virtualized&#8221;, existing limits will dissolve, and new limitations of virtual organizations will arise. This implies, especially for the regions, that new chances evolve which should be used for an objective-orientated realization. In this contribution two examples of such virtualized and regional networks as well as one model for a systematic procedure are outlined
