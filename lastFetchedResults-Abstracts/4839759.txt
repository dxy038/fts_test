The article sets up the objective weights of multiattribute estimation using the entropy theory, and confirms its synthetical weights with the subjective judgment, so that a multiattribute estimation model is built up. Then using this model, it analyses the 5 typical student loan scheme in the world of Hong kong, China, USA, Canada, Thailand,and calculates the synthetical weights of every attribute as well as the total estimation result of every country student loan scheme, which indicates the arrangement of every attribute weights in student loan scheme. Based on the arrangement, it also proposes some material policy suggestions to improve the student loan scheme in our country.
