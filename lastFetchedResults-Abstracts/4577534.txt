The rapid development and popularity of Internet provides a good chance for postal tuition of fire officers and soldiers. B/S model was adopted in an online examination system on fire postal tuition and the article was going to be written that based on ASP and ACCESS database. System mainly realizes the functions such as online answer, automatic scoring, examinee archives maintenance, online management question bank, making test plan online, random out of the volume of objective questions. The system principles, the system function design, key technologies realized were described separately combined with a specific design task.
