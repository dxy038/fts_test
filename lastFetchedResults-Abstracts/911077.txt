The main objective of this study is identifying the rate of passive defence consideration
and operational impact on traffic management. This study with the aim of analyzing
threat concept, crisis and passive defence; and studing the rate of passive defence impact
on Tehran crisis management is encountered with a question which is entitled as «are
passive defence operations effective on traffic management and population evacuation
at the time of crisis?». This study is applicable and its data statistical data include Tehran
road managers, experts and officers. Simple sampling is based on the cockran formula.
The results of the analysis indicated that passive defence factors including identifying
population size and operation in space, appropriate distribution of land uses against
threat, locating land uses and defensive crisis management on population evacuation at
the time of crisis have effective impact in Tehran
