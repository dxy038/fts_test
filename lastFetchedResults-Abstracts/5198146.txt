Food enterprises integrate supply chain management and e-commerce, in order to form complete e-supply chain. It is a better mode of promoting food businesses to develop rapidly. Food enterprises are rather unfamiliar with the use of e-commerce in China. The article points out the necessity of integrating e-commerce for food enterprises, starting with the analysis of four stages of developing e-commerce. With the analysis of advantages and deficiencies of food enterprises developing e-commerce, the article discusses objective requirement for food enterprises and proposes the countermeasures based on the present situation of food industry.
