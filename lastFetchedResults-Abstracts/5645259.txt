Along with the continuous development of Internet, business activities through networks are becoming more and more common, yet completing a deal requires a lot of inquiry about and comparison of different business Web sites to go through matchmaking and negotiation with many trading partners. This complexity greatly obstructs the development of e-commerce. In order to realize the integration and share of resources and services, this paper proposes the dynamic electronic tradeoff model that is active service-oriented and applies the techniques of agent and Web service. And the paper demonstrates the designing principle of the active service-oriented electronic tradeoff model, and the in-depth study on design of the model´s architecture, modelling and solution of the multilateral multi-objective matchmaking agent and the bilateral multi-objective negotiation agent, and the experimental simulation of the related algorithms, and proposes the author´s own opinion on this study.
