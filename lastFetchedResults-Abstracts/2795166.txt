The practical teaching is an important part in innovation and entrepreneurship education carried out by colleges, it is necessary to analyze and comprehensively utilize all aspects of the practical teaching of innovation and entrepreneurship education in order to make innovation and entrepreneurship education implemented effectively in colleges. Based on the characteristics of high practicality and applicability of engineering specialty, by combining innovation and entrepreneurship education with specialized education, the practical teaching system for engineering college students in innovation and entrepreneurship education with effectiveness and portability was constructed which consisted of objective system, content system, security system and management system.
