Given the importance of globalization in the present study we have analyzed the challenges facing our state agencies to deal with globalization. In this regard, the four ministries at random as the sample in this study we have examined. The main objective of this study is to identify and prioritize the challenges facing our state organizations, and to use managers and researchers commentaries in the field of public administration so that these organizations would be able to privies the important challenges ahead and to obtain clearer perspective in decision - making. To conduct this research, we first review the literature on globalization and also conducted a number of interviews with experts, Academic researchers to integrate, remove or add the challenges and we finally got to the final composition. The challenges were grouped using factor analysis and got rankings by fuzzy TOPSIS methods. Finally a number of general guidelines to deal with the main challenges were presented to four of the eight dimensions.
