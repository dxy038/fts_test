The main objective of this article is to analyze factors affected the participation of faculty
members in scientific and research activities and investigation of barriers. The research
design was a descriptive and correlation surveying method. Faculty members of Islamic
Azad University, Shoushtar Branch were the target population for this study. By census
method all of population were surveyed (N=43). The main result of the study revealed that
there was significant correlation between the interested in scientific and research activities,
rate of using web sites in internet, relationship with research centers, age, educational
level, interested in educational activities with level of participation of faculty members in
scientific and research activities. The variables interested in scientific and research
activities, rate of using web sites in internet, relationship with research centers and
educational level explained for 61 percent of changes in participation of faculty members
in scientific and research activities
