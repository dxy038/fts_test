This paper constructed land use sustainable evaluation model based on comprehensive ecological footprint and AHP. First, the thesis introduced ecological footprint analysis method and some related conceptions, then based on AHP and comprehensive EF constructed land use sustainable evaluation index system and model which was composed of three objectives, six sub-objective and seventeen indexes and was referred to ecological, economic and social aspects, finally, applied the model to Shandong province and concluded the defect and prospect of this model. By evaluating the dynamic change of land use sustainable degree of Shandong province, we found that the trend of land use sustainable degree growly reduced and the prospect was pessimistic, so strict measures should be taken to promote land use sustainability.
