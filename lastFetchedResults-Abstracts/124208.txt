To prove the truth of "the Quranic sciences", the discovering of the relationships between the Quran's laws is required. The main objective of the present essay is required. The main objective of the present essay is to show the invariable relationship between action and reaction in life as one of the most important of these laws. This study leads to enhancements in individual and social motivations and deeds. To achieve this end the following method is adopted. Firstly, two premises are provided: A) a full study of deeds in the Quran, and B) explanation of the relationships between deeds and their consequences. Secondly, in order to improve the discussion, we divided the Qurans's verses into two groups: A) those explicitly mentioning the action and its consequences, and B) those deductively or inductively implying this relationship.
