One of the essentials of administrative virtue in the police organization is precise inspection by the police inspection unit to have a psychological sense of safety and to institutionalize the proper treatment of clients. This psychological sense of safety and proper treatment of clients can have functional, behavioral, structural and environmental components. This paper's objective is to analyze the proper implementation of administrative virtue guidelines focusing on the role of its key factors based on monitoring by the inspection and surveillance sector of police, and demonstrate the degree of the inspection and surveillance sectorâ€™s effect on the key factors and the police administrative virtue. This work used various perspectives on the evaluation of administrative virtue and through establishing relationship between these perspectives and the factors suggests an overall solution suitable for the police. The results indicated that administrative virtue with a focus on the inspections could convert the principles that have been mentioned to the values.
