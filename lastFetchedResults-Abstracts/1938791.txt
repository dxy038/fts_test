Discussion of sensitive goods has a relatively long history in wto negotiations. Usually countries discuss trade, while giving different privileges, including reducing tariffs and tariff barriers and increasing access to the market for the trade in goods for which they consider to be the most important and sensitive. 
Given the importance of these commodities the present paper tries to provide the proposed methodology to determine the sensitive products.
To achieve this objective our approach is based on performance indicators and statistical information available at six - figure HS codes, including the tariff, the value of exports and imports, indicators of Specilizatin in exports and imports, terms of trade index. 
Also it Unctad method that was used in a study for providing sensitive products in India has been used.
