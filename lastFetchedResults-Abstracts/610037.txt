In this article, the settings of formation of “reformism discourse” and its meaning system during years 1997-2007 are analyzed. Based on analytical-documental method, this research shows that in reformism discourse in spite of some positive achievements in the realm of political participation, there has been one dimensional attitude towards political development. Therefore some important aspects such as economic problems, justice and values are ignored. These matters have caused in instability and disorders. In this space, some obstacles have been rise against political development and religious democracy.
