To foster a deep objective evaluation that the import and export trade plays a important role of Heilongjiang Provincial economic growth, this paper are analyzed the status of import and export trade of Heilongjiang Province, where they are introduced based on the International Trade Theory and the Theory of Economic Growth. Finally, policy recommendations are made, based on empirical analysis by the combination of the actual situation in Heilongjiang province, which will provide some help to research into the future development of the import and export trade of Heilongjiang Province.
