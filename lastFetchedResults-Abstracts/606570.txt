Profit is one of the main objectives of the financial management of the company. It is not only
objective, but growth for the company. It is not usually pay for the invested capital, but an
instrument which no one company can not developt;at the same time gain evidence of the rationality
of governance, the success of the activity and the risk that entrepreneurs take when investing.
Profitability is the entity's ability to "produce" profit.The aim of this study was to analyze profit and
profitability of a particular company - "Biser Oliva", Stara Zagora and on this basis to give
recommendations for increasing profitability.
