By introducing the Cobb-Douglas demand function and the cross-price elasticity of the skiing park, the relation between the requirement of the consumers and the unit-price has been established. With the profit of the skiing park owner as the objective function, the conditions of the maximizing the profit have been presented and the prices of all the service grading when the function of profit takes the maximum have been given. It is significant for making the most rational prices of the skiing park and maintaining the behalf of both the owner and the consumers.
