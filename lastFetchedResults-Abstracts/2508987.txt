Regarding the issue of security in all aspects including important things that must be considered in the design of the built environment is the city planners and designers. The aim of this study was to investigate the causes of insecurity in urban areas Yasouj, this urban spaces, including streets, sidewalks, streets, squares, entertainment centers and municipal facilities and equipment is.
This study is based on objective and applications based on cross-sectional nature of the desired information from multiple sources as library, documents and field visits have been collected. The assessment tool is a researcher-made questionnaire among 100 residents of the town of Yasouj randomly distributed and the results were analyzed using SPSS software.
