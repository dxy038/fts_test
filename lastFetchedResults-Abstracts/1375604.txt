In the advanced economic and social systems, there are trade and professional organizations in various fields. Each component and element of these organizations are familiar with the role and responsibilities and scope of its activities. Appropriate relationship between components is defined as purposeful and in line with the main objective. This paper describes current situation in the world and our country of the industry of packaging. Also this paper explains reasons a bout the necessity of forming and consolidation activities in the packaging. Keywords: consolidation, packaging, the identity.
