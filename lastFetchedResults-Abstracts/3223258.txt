Matheepot Phattanasak, Roghayeh Gavagsaz-Ghoachani, Jean-Philippe Martin, Bobak Nahid-Mobarakeh, Serge Pierfederici, and Bernard Davat of the University of Lorraine, France have received the award of the best paper on renewable energies, for their paper entitled: "Control of a Hybrid Energy Source Comprising a Fuel Cell and Two Storage Devices using Isolated Three-Port Bidirectional DC-DC Converters" (EVER13-119).
