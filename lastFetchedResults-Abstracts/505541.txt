Improving the quality of life is the main objective of all plannings. The presence of problems necessitates the study of the quality of life. This study is a survey based on questionnaire. Its main objective is to measure the variables related to the quality of life and their impacts on the security of border areas in Nosoud district of Kermanshah province. The study results indicated that the quality of life in the area under study is not high and among the variables three of them, passing leisure time, quality of environment and physical quality have influenced the security of border areas.
