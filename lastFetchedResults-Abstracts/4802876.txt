This paper applies the fuzzy reasoning into the process of the forestry pests and diseases´s diagnosis. Firstly, it introduces the fuzzy reasoning and the expert system, and then a new design method is proposed, which can carries on the objective quantitative analysis and effective processing to each kind of indefinite information. At last this method is taken into application and The practice indicates that it can enhances forestry pests and diseases expert system´s reliability and the intelligence.
