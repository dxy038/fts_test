Most service providers in the telecommunications market are facing the major business transformation challenges, such as:- offering a more flexible service choice to the service customer, -providing a more customer involved role in the service, -increasing the quality of communication between the customer and service provider, -increasing service availability and providing customer self-help in telecommunication and -reducing the service operation cost. Customer self-service is a way to provide all these benefits for the customer and to help service provider reduce his operation costs.
