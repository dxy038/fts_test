This article provides a critical scrutiny of the growing scholarly writings on Iran's nuclear program, debunking the paradigmatic certainty of various works regarding Iran's "nuclear ambition." this is followed by a detailed examination of the history and chronology of Iran's nuclear diplomacy, covering such issues as Iran's compliance with the IAEA standards, Iran-EU negotiations, the question of objective guarantees, etc. The article concludes by calling for "good faith" negotiations in order to settle the current disputes on Iran's nuclear program.
