Along with urbanization, population growth, the problem of domestic refuse has become the major problem that we have to face. Through the comparison of the domestic refuse treatment planning between Changchun and Shenzhen, this paper analyses the existing problem in domestic refuse treatment of Changchun and contracts with the newest strategy of domestic refuse, which is putted foreword according to the latest overall objective of urban development in Shenzhen. At this time, this paper proposes optimizative suggestions, which suit local condition, for Changchun domestic refuse treatment planning.
