With the objective of adding state-of-the-art facilities to the energy management system (EMS), China Light and Power Company (CLP) of Hong Kong is replacing the existing system with a new EMS. In this new EMS, the computer processors are more powerful. Together with enhanced software it will achieve an overall improvement in system capacity and performance.
