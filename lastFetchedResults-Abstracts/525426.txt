This article is to provide an overview for assessing and ranking e-businesses using TOPSIS Method. The main objective of this article is to gather a comparatively comprehensive collection of criteria for assessing and ranking e-businesses. At first, e-business, e-business models, its environment and environmental pressures in using e-business are explained. Then, e-businesses assessing and ranking criteria are classified. Finally an example using TOPSIS method for ranking ten companies in rendered.
