This paper constructs the athletes´ life cycle-sustainable livelihoods framework (LC-SLF) and analyzes the transform of the athletes´ sustainable livelihoods assets under background of the life cycle. Empirical study on the influence factors of the sustainable livelihoods assets is carried out, based on the investigation data of Liaoning athletes. The experimental results show that the factors of social security system, educational level, social attention and sports development have obviously positive effects on the athletes´ sustainable livelihoods of assets formationg. So, the advice has been put forward to improve the optimal allocation of the athletes´ sustainable livelihoods assets by aiming at sustainable livelihood as ultimate objective of the security policy, improving educational mechanism, reforming distribution system of athletes intangible assets, designing compensatory payment mechanism, completing athletes social security system and establishing a multi-tier social support network.
