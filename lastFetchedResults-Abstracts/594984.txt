Objective: Objective is to describe the natural history of colliod cysts and their reliable radiological diagnosis.Material and Methods: Patients presenting with headache, vomiting fits etc from January 2001 to August 2003 in department of Radiology, Postgraduate Medical Institute, Lady Reading Hospital, Peshawar were investigated by CT Head taking axial section at 10 mm distance form base to vertex.Results: Two cases of colloid cyst of third ventricular and diagnosed on CT Scan.Conclusion: Colloid cyst of the third ventricular is a rare disease whosediagnosis can be relied on CT Scan.
