Circular economy is a new concept of sustainable development model of economic development with the objective of making the most effective use of resources and protecting the environment. Green Logistics is the achievement of circular economy in the logistics field. From the demand perspective, green transportation should be environment-friendly transportation and resources-saved transportation. Besides, we should establish a scientific and effective system of dangerous goods transportation for early warning and emergency response. As to management of green transportation, the government departments should fully apply various measures to protecting the green transport links, companies should vigorously develop third party logistics to achieve the implementation of combined transportation, the development of joint distribution and the optimization to bring about circular economy and benefit.
