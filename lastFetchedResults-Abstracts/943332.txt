The ultimate aim of training in Islam is to make man Godly and to bring him close to perfect beauty. Therefore, it seems quite essential to identify the real boundaries of beauty. Analyzing the training aspects of the concept of beauty in the descriptive compound words of the holy Quran, as the most valid and reliable source in Islam- is a step toward lightening the way to reach this purpose. Conceptual analysis method along with comparison is applied to this research. The results indicate that the concept of beauty in the compound descriptive terms of the holy Quran covers individual, interpersonal, as well as sensual and abstract areas. Accordingly, the educational indications of the Quranic terms relating to beauty concern issues covering individual, interpersonal, objective, and abstract beauties.
