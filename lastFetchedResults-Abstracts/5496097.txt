Associating with the feasibility in configuration-issued platform model of SIS system in power station, this thesis provides a complete SVG graphic format transfer plan. The searching objective of this article is EDPF-NT+ distributed control system graphic document. It employs a way to parse the elements respectively. This thesis also makes an elaborate explanation on the technical problem of graphic transfer. The significance of this method in the theory is profound, and the engineering usage of it can be highly appreciated.
