The article refer to the problems in which measurement about urban residential areas lighting, on the basis of collecting related data and results in the field, and adopt the methods of intersecting architecture, urban planning, automation, computers, developing a suit of "measurement hardware system of urban residential areas lighting based on a platform for remote control vehicle". The system can exactly and efficiently detect parameters such as illumination and brightness, at the same time, it can comprehensive and objective detect luminous environment, and supply technology endorsement on discovery for developing lighting standards of urban residential.
