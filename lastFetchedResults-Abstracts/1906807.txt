The main objective of the present study was to evaluate the effect of implementing education management model based on re-engineering approach in social studies on the improvement of learning and attitudes of girl’s high school freshman in Bint al-Huda Shahriar in 93-92 academic year. Two classes with the size of 30 students for each one were selected randomly through feasibility of access method and the model and ordinal method were used as the independent variable for both. The teacher made pre-test assessed the previous knowledge of students and the posttest compared the results of the two teaching approach. Students’ attitude was also assessed through a teacher-made questionnaire. Analysis of data acquired was done through using ANCOVA and the result made it secures to recommend the model for improving learning and attitudes of students in teaching the other subjects.
