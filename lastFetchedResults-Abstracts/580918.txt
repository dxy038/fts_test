Abstract
In this paper according to the scientific basics of SCM in the military section, which relies on Maximizing the weakness and maximizing power of friendly forces enemy, we provided a model due to evaluating and selecting ammunition providers. 
In accordance to this reason, after diagnosing and providing provides with designing model and using MADM   method, it is mention their method of evaluation in Asymmetric battles.
The objective of this study summarizing in choosing suitable ammunition provider and boosting SCM, varied from producting industries to armed forces, upon a doctrine of Asymmetric battles.
