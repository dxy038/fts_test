In the Software College of Shandong University, embedded system education is one of the characteristic subjects, so "practical training" is the brand new characters as the most important process in the embedded system education. Practical training is an on-campus process to gain the practical experiences which are suitable for the real IT companies, especially for the students without work experiences. The steps of practical training include objective determinations, member organizations, corresponding project managements and developments, writing the supported courseware or the relative textbooks. Practical training has spread to most of the college students, including undergraduate students and the new coming MSEs.
