Abstract
This research has been done for studying and explaining the relationship between of psychological empowerment dimensions with personnel creativity. The research is descriptive from objective approach  and data collection method is survey research and from data analysis method approach is correlation research. The statistical population is the personnel in Engineering and Technical Service firms in Tehran and selected 200 firms as statistical sample by using of judgment sampling of nonprobability sampling methods. It is used from questionnaire for collecting the required data of psychological empowerment e dimensions and personnel creativity and used Pierson correlation and multi regression methods for analyzing data. Results show there is right and strong correlation between psychological empowerment dimensions with personnel creativity. Also, the share of psychological empowerment of employee dimensions in predicting personnel creativity is, approximately, 51 percent.
