The Russian Trading System Technical Centre (RTS TC) was founded in October 1995 as a technical support centre to operate the RTS. Its main objective is to provide financial markets operators with technological tools to perform trading, manage front office and back office activities, organize the transmission of the data between the trading system, broker-dealer firms, clearing and settlement institutions, control authorities and to the public. The article describes the system and in particular its user interface-the information provided, its layout, the style of interaction, communication methods, and alerts
