The study of the women´s competitive gymnastics, picking out 60 young women whose bodies are close to the physiological index of competitive gymnastics athletes to try on. Using the methods of the subjective and objective experiments, the paper makes a research into pressure comfort when people try on seamless gymnastics clothing in the stillness and movement conditions. Applying the parameter interval estimation method to get main body parts´ scope of pressure comfort about women´s seamless gymnastics clothing, including chest, shoulder, arm, armpit, elbow, back, waist, abdomen and buttocks, to gain functional relation between the clothing pressure of key parts and their respective ample amounts, so it can provide each part of clothing for size optimization and reference for the design of the gymnastics clothing.
