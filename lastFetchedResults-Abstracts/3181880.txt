Protection of natural forest is very important, the development of natural forest protection project region that implementing the natural forest protection project is also important. Taking the natural forest protection project region as research object and with an objective of coordinated development between natural forest protection and socio-economic development, this paper puts forward theory for coordinated development between resources environment and socio-economic of natural forest protection project region based on analyzing the situation of coordinated development between resources environment and socio-economic of that region.
