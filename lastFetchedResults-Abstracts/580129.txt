This research has been done to study the effect of competitive strategy on foreign performance of engineering and technical services exporting firms. The research because of its objective and nature, is a descriptive research and the survey is dependent on data collection method. The statistical population comprises of 41 engineering and technical services exporting firms as statistical sample by judgment sampling of nonprobability sampling methods. This sample covers 95 percent of Iran engineering and technical exporting services. Questionnaires were used to collect the required data and variance analysis and multi regression methods were applied for analyzing data. The results show that the competitive strategy has a considerable effect on foreign performance of engineering and technical services firms. Also, the portion of competitive strategy in predicting and changes of foreign performance of engineering and technical services firms is 51 percent.
