The present paper is a result of an empirical research to study the relationship between negative (revolutionary) participation for establishment and positive (electoral) participation to stabilize and strengthen the Islamic Republic system. The objective of this study has been investigated through a documentary study through comparative method inside the country with county as the unit of analysis. The popular struggles of the people during the 18 months leading to the victory of the revolution were analyzed in cross-sectional method to study the revolutionary participation. And the period between 1989 to 2005 has been investigated for an analysis of electoral participation. Finally, the findings of the research have been described through statistical techniques in the form of static and dynamic reports.
