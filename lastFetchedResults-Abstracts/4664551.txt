In the paper,the risk problem of reverse logistics of retailing is discussed by measure model. In the national economy, it is essential for risk assessment of reverse logistics of retailing to decide effectively. It is an important method to reduce operation risk and improve service level. In this paper, firstly, the risk assessment of reverse logistics of retailing is analyzed. And then, the effect factors of reverse logistics are finished and classed. At last, the objective risk cases of reverse logistics are analyzed quantitatively to account for the fact that Several Indexes unascertained comprehensive evaluation is a scientific effective and strongly operational method.
