The integration among Islamic countries had been pursued in few
past centuries. The major objective of this article is to examine the
problems associated with the policy making in the area of
integration of member states of the Organization of Islamic
Countries (OIC). The investigation is aimed at finding a suitable
answer to the following question: Considering the major
developments in international security environment, has the
success of integrating policies become more crucial for the OIC?
Why the OIC cannot attain the real integration between Islamic
Countries? This article studied the external factors: political,
economical, social-cultural issues & also internal challenges of
OIC.
