Under the support of the computer technique and the software of GIS, It forms a software support systems for solution to the land use planning implementary evaluation, according to the mathematical model and the specific processes of implementary evaluation. The system provides user-selectable menu for various view of operations and information processing, the system has a friendly interface, simple operation, rapid and get results, greatly reducing the workload of evaluators to improve the working efficiency. The system is used in the implementation of evaluation of the overall plan for land use empirical analysis for Hebi City, the results show that the system can be operable, and the evaluation results in line with objective reality, in order to implement the evaluation of the overall planning of land use provide a good auxiliary and support.
