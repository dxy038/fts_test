In most security domain transmission of secret and confidential voice data requires that the identity of the speaker should not get revealed but at the same time data contents in speech signal should be interpreted. Speech and speaker normalization techniques help to solve this issue. This paper proposes speaker normalization technique that attempts to alter the characteristics of speakerÂ´s voice so that it would be difficult for an eavesdropper to identify the speaker. Intended listener can retrieve the particular information. Evaluations of subjective and objective tests confirm the effectiveness of the proposed method and because of this method security enhances.
