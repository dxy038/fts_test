Based on detailed discussion of enterprise ecological relationships in economics development zone, this paper fully described the relationships and evolution processes between enterprises, and established a dynamic co-evolution model for enterprises in economics development zone by reference of Lotka-Volterra model to analyze the co-evolution motion and mechanism for enterprises in economics development zone. The objective is to provide a reliable reference and method for the enterprise that how to increase the ability of organizational survival and improve the existing strategy in the existent competitive paradigm and how to select strategy and realize the organization adoptive transform when the competitive paradigm changed, and then to strengthen the whole competitive ability of industrial chain.
