Objective: In this research, we present a framework which has been introduced for improving the efficiency of requirement change management in GSD environment. Method: In this research, we have used structured, semi structured research methods gained through interviews from reputed organization experts. This research is based on published reports, articles, and existing case studies.Experts reviews are used to perform the evaluation of this model. Result: This has resulted in a framework for improving the effectiveness of requirement change management in GSD environment. Conclusion: The framework provides a guideline to the team of developers regarding how frequently changes could be incorporated.
