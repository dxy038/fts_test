This paper proposes Web-based solver for 9&#215;9 and 16&#215;16 and Sudoku puzzles, namely SudokuWeb. Current available Web-based Sudoku puzzles provide the problems of puzzles so that a user can solve it manually. In case that the level of a Sudoku problem is high, it may take more than half an hour to solves manually. This research is combination of using GNU linear programming kit (GLPK) and Web-server to act as a SudokuWeb solver. Our objective is to provide a Web-based solver to solve any user-defined Sudoku puzzles.
