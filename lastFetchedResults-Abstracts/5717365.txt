Loan risk is one of most important source of risk that our commercial bank face and it constructs the main part of risk management. So strengthening the measurement is of much significance to our commercial bank. Based on brief introduction of the theory of loan projects risk, considering the complexity of multi-objective decision of credit risk assessment in commercial bank, this paper builds a new analysis index system for the loan projects risk ranking in commercial bank. By using GRA method to determine the weights of the evaluating indicators and taking the fuzzy comprehensive evaluation as the evaluation criteria, it advances a new method of loan projects risk assessment in commercial bank. The practical examples show that this method is feasible.
