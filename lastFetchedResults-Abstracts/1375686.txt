Health and Hygiene aspect of plastics used in packaging food industry


In food packaging, the main objective is protection and increase shelf life of products. Plastic is a kind of polymers, can be formed under pressure and low temperature. They are light, cheap and strong. So they have beautiful appearance.



 


During production and process of plastics maybe happen some problems.
Such as use of unauthorized additives, or increase the amount of additives,
These cases can be created health and hygiene risks.
This article will express standards and important points in this issue.
