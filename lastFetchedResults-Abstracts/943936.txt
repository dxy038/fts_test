This study explains similarities and differences of hope defined by Quran viewpoint and by speeches with positivist psychology using descriptive-analytic method. Hope meaning achievement to goal accompanied by effort, goal as main and key component of hope, and designing the affairs for achieving desirable goals, are similarity aspects. Also, divine ideology against individual ideology, dynamic and model-designing theory against neutral and non-value-oriented theory and comprehensive methods against objective methods are difference aspects. Study results states that hope in education from viewpoint of Quran and Islamic instructions are more comprehensive than pessimistic and positivist psychology.
