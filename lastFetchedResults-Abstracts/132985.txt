It is the main objective of this paper to present the truth of science and the unity of the wise and wisdom on the basis of the books written by scholars.
The present paper is comprised of four parts, namely definition of the nature of science, the nature of human perception, description of the true meaning of "unity of the wise and the wisdom", and finally, the manner of recollection following amnesia.
With regard to each and every part of the discussion, viewpoints of Molla-Sadra will be introduced and then it will be compared with those of other scholars such as Ebn-e Sina so that final conclusions can be arrived at.
