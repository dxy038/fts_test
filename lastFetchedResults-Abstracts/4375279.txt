As a central city in the western area of China and the upriver area of Yangtse, Chongqing has been endowed with many preferential policies by government. This paper tries to develop two kinds of mode suitable for Chongqing to develop urban-rural balanced bidirectional logistic system, which may be helpful to change the dual structure and hence harmonize the development of the urban and the rural. The concrete mode includes the bilateral logistic system mode encircling the manufacturing enterprise and the bilateral logistic system mode based on chain stores, whose procedure of operation are illustrated clearly respectively, which is surely referable for Chongqing to construct urban-rural integrated logistic system.
