The main objective of the present study was to identify the indexes of rural tourism development with emphasis on the cultural tourism in the village of Heidarabad in Ilam. The Present research was a quantitative study as far as the methodology is concerned and a survey one if its data collection is regarded. The statistical population of this study consisted of three groups of expert, rural households and tourists. In order to choose the sample, the researcher used census method for the first group; convenience sampling method for tourists. Totally 250 people were chosen for the study.  The main instrument of gathering data was questionnaire the validity of which was confirmed by a group of experts. The Cronbach alpha Coefficient used to determine questionnaire reliability was estimated 0.96. The results of factor analysis showed that regarding cultural dimension, the promotional mechanisms with the percentage of 21.93 was the most important mechanism of rural tourism development.
