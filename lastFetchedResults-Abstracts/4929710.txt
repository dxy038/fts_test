This research discusses the prevention solution Tuberculosis (TB) in Indonesia through a reminder electronic health system that is designed to adopt mobile cloud computing technologies, so TB organization can have access and take advantage information technology resources without making a major investment, including for patients to be easy to get immediate treatment from their mobile devices. The results of this research is the design and prototype of a Software as a service (SaaS) in the Mobile Cloud environment for the management of TB treatment that can support the principle of integration, harmonization and sustainability of e-health system of TB in Indonesia.
