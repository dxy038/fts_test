Networking behavior is considered a new approach among interdisciplinary concepts related to entrepreneurship field of study but so far few attempts have been made to investigate its relationship with entrepreneurial behavior. From this perspective, the main objective of this article is to study the relationship between the networking behavior and the entrepreneurial behavior. This survey research examines the role of variables among 137 presidents of companies located in the Science Parks of the University of Tehran, Amir Kabir University of Technology and Tarbiat Modarres University.The data have been gathered by standard questionnaires and the statistics has been analyzed using Pearsonâ€™s correlation coefficient and step-by-step regression.The results show that there is a direct relationship between the networking behavior and the entrepreneurial behavior and the dimensions of building, maintaining and using of the networking behavior, have the most impact on entrepreneurial behavior respectively.
