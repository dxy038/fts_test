Accurate measurement depends on many aspects of the measurement system being correct and the correct use of leads and connectors are fundamental in achieving this objective. The equipment now used in much of industry is modern and of a very high standard, but how it is connected to the unknown device to make measurements has often been a major concern-the standard of leads and connectors in use has been far from ideal.
