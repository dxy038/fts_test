Creativity is recognition and expression of the problem and providing new solutions for it.Studies show that human beings prefer application of creative methods in learning process.Teaching patterns on the basis of creativity have significant effect on successful education. But, unfortunately the same studies indicate that these methods are the least one that have been used in classroom because teachers usually prefer dictating of information in learning method.The objective of this study is upgrading teaching by application of creative teaching patterns.The method of this survey is librarystudies.Colleting of data and information is performed by using of different references such as documents,books and published articles in this field.In this article, the concept of creativity and the necessity of nurturing it among learners and creative teaching guidelines are described.Finally,those teaching patterns have been analyzed that lead to nurturing creativity.
