Body descriptions in Shahnamah are not limited to aesthetic aspects. But are used as a style to show pschyclogical situations of stories' heros and what is known as characterization. In this regard, Ferdowsi is considered to present a personal style, as Persian poetry in the Korasani style is objective and free from subjective stance.
In this paper the body descriptions are studied from a new viewpoint based on non-verbal communications or body language.
