This paper describes our approach to assured information sharing. The research is being carried out under a MURI 9Multiuniversity Research Initiative) project funded by the air force office of scientific research (AFOSR). The main objective of our project is: define, design and develop an assured information sharing lifecycle (AISL) that realizes the DoDÂ´s information sharing value chain. In this paper we describe the problem faced by the department of defense and our solution to developing an AISL system.
