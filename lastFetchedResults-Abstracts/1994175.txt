The main objective of this research is to find out the elements of cultural training from Imam Khomeiniâ€™s viewpoint. This research follows an inferential-inductive analytical method. It applies a documentary method by reviewing the writings of Imam Khomeini for data collection. The results indicated that cultural training is the basis of virtue or wickedness according to Imam Khomeini. The salvation of all societies depends on cultural revival and modification. Cultural training aims at promotion and improvement of insight toward better understanding of divine culture and values. It also aims at gaining conscious selection of divine culture. The elements of such training include self-reliance, self-consciousness, defiance, local-culture, and cultural independence.
