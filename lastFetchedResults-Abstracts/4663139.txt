The following topics are dealt with:fuzzy-inference based neural network model; genetic fuzzy expert system; constraint retranslation; multi-objective optimization model; fuzzy linear systems; fuzzy probability theory; rule extraction algorithm; random fuzzy multiobjective programming; adaptive fuzzy control; adaptive k-nearest neighbor algorithm; response abilities of time-varying fuzzy systems; approximating the true domain of fuzzy inference sentence; BL-general fuzzy automata; decomposition theorems and representation theorems of lattice interval value fuzzy sets; finite-time stabilization of a class of uncertain nonlinear systems with time-delay; fuzzy decision based combination model for securities investment; and generalizing Dempster-Shafer evidence theory.
