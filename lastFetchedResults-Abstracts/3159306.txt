Operating municipal infrastructure projects are a special operation model that the Government and the project investors achieve the operating infrastructure projects by signing infrastructure concession contracts. This paper is aimed at the feature of operating infrastructure projects to achieve the objective of maximizing the value of the projects, under the conditions the concession period and the concession price given by the Government. This paper uses the Nash negotiation model to design optimization models of financing structure of operating municipal infrastructure projects, and provide reference for making strategic financing decisions of the practical operating municipal infrastructure projects.
