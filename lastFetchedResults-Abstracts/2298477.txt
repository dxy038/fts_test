Innovation is necessary for achieving competitive advantages and long term success of all organizations and companies. At the beginning of 21st century, and with the intensification of competition and globalization trend, the importance of this issue has increased. Although there are a lot of studies and researches in this field, most of them were done in manufacturing industry, therefore, there are few researches on innovation management in constructing contractor Industry. 
The main objective of this study is to identify the effective factors on innovation management in construction industry. In order to do this, we used case study research method and five construction contractor companies were selected (as cases), and their data and information were collected by triangle methods. The collected data was analyzed by grounded theory method. The results of this study are presented in the form of a method for innovation management in construction contractor companies. At the end, we proposed some suggestions for future research.
