An important objective of the EuroTraining project (GA No.: 316526 - Funded by the European Union) is to promote networking and international cooperation activities in the field of university level education of nanoelectronics and micro-/nano-systems. An Internet search was carried out to identify and categorize the highest level global networks in this field. The contribution summarizes the results, presents some examples, and provides the links how to search for and access the identified and qualified networks.
