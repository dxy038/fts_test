Objective(s): The purpose of this study was to measure patients' satisfaction of medical care delivered in outpatient clinics of endocrinology and metabolism in Firoozgar teaching hospital Tehran, Iran. Methods: This was a cross-sectional study. One hundred and fifty-nine patients participated in the study. A self-administered questionnaire was employed. The questions consisted of seven domains: general satisfaction, technical quality, interpersonal aspect, communication, financial aspect, time spent with doctor, access, availability and convenience.
