In order to reduce the influence of the subjectivity and evaluate the competitiveness of the e-commerce website effectively, this paper applies entropy method to establish model to determine the objective weight of each index, and calculates the distance and close-degree between the e-commerce website and the ideal point, and then sorts the competitiveness of the e-commerce websites according to the close-degree. The results show that the method can provide strong support to evaluate the competitiveness of e-commerce websites.
