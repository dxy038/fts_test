River erosion has a lot of damage on soil and water resource . The erosion rate in Iran is
about 1 to 2,5 Mton/ha/year. It shows a crisis in our country. Vegetation cover is one of
the important factors on erosion control. The main objective of present study is to know: How were rivers in study area controlled by vegetation and what are kinds of
vegetation? The results shows small trees, shrubs and trees are the best space in order
on lateral erosion control.
