This work present a educational experience about digital narrative with mobile devices and emotional education developed at the Art School &#8220;Pancho Lasso&#8221;, inside the Language and Audiovisual Media cycle. The objective of this experience was to work on the specific competences of audiovisual communication and creation of specific interactive products trough the creation of digital narratives, and at the same time working on transversal key competences such as emotional, technological and social abilities.
