The 2nd IEEE Asia-Pacific Conference on ASIC (AP-ASIC), hosted by the IEEE ED/SSC Seoul Chapter, was held at Hotel Shilla Cheju, Cheju, Korea, 27&#8211;30 August 2000. The objective of AP-ASIC is to promote research and development activities of solid-state circuits in the Asia-Pacific region. AP-ASIC 2000 featured 2 tutorials, 3 keynote speeches, 4 embedded tutorials, and 97 presented papers (83 papers in 17 regular sessions and 14 papers in 3 special sessions). The total number of registrants from five countries (Korea, Taiwan, Japan, the United States, and India) was 170 and there were 22 tutorial attendants.
