This book is very well written and includes the following: fundamental principles of systems; new approaches for solving classic operation/production systems; multicriteria decision making (MCDM) and multiple-objective optimization (MOO) formulations for all classic operations and production problems; several methods for solving MCDM and MOO problems; and Z utility theory application for MCDM problems, decision paradoxes, and descriptive risk problems. The book has 19 chapters, presented in seven parts.
