The personalized demands of contemporary college students has been increasing with the development of information technology in teaching of P.E, thus, how to provide students with personalized physical education (P.E) course has become particularly urgent. This paper starts with the analysis of college studentsÂ´ demands for P.E classes, proposes the concept of consulting system for university athletic sports, and analyzes the functions and processes of the personalized recommendation system for P.E course at universities. Then, this paper describes the designing results and proposes the system application architecture. The main objective of the consulting system is to recommend physical education course for students, give sports advice to them, as well as to provide decision support for rational allocation of teaching resources of schools.
