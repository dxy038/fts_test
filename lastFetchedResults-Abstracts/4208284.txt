This paper describes three organizations that helps the development of the power sector in Central Africa, namely: Central African Power Pool (CAPP), Economic Community of Central African States (ECCAS) and the New Partnership for AfricaÂ´s Development (NEPAD). CAPP vision is to exploit the enormous hydroelectric potentialities of the Central Africa and to satisfy all demands in electricity with the households, the states and the central African industry. ECCAS promotes and reinforces a harmonious cooperation and a dynamic, balance and auto-kept development in all domains of the economic and social activity. NEPAD objective is to guarantee the lasting development and the integration of the African continent.
