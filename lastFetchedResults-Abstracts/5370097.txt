Business financial strategy includes funding strategy, investment strategy, and income distribution strategy. Because different financial strategies are interrelated and mutually influenced, the conduction of financial strategic risk is objective and complex. Firstly, this paper introduces the definition of the conduction of financial strategic risk, then analyzes three elements in the conduction: risk sources, the vector of conduction and the fuse of conduction. In the conduction of financial strategic risk, the various uncertain factors of internal and external environment are risk sources of financial strategic risk, cash flow is the vector of financial strategic riskÂ´s conduction, and threshold mutation is the fuse in the conduction of financial strategic risk.
