Mycetoma is a chronic and pustular infection usually involves
foot. Mycetoma in Iran is not common and most cases have
been reported from the South and North of Jran. The objective
of present study was to review the mycetom a and the distribution
of its etiologic agents in Iran. Moreover, a 60-year-old
woman with actinomycetoma lesions in her left foot was presented.
Direct smear of the grains showed positive, non-acid
fast and branching filaments of actinomycetes. Negative result
was obtained from the cultures of grains.
