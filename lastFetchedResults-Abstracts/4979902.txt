The objective of this paper is to outline and describe the process of developing team councils in a wafer fab organization. Initially, we present the historical background and why we thought this type of approach was necessary to achieve high performance from all levels of the organization. A road map to success as well as a task level migration matrix describes the different levels of responsibility needed to achieve the results described in the conclusion of this paper.
