The main objective of this study is to explore how to design the user interfaces of handheld device based on Android. The methodology of UI design based on Android is summarized through discussing the different tasks of three representative roles -requirements analyst; UI designer; software engineer in the life cycle of software development. Most of the current research is to discuss the realization of the software applications based on the Android platform. There are limited studies on UI design from the perspective of the different development stages of the entire project. This paper focuses on regulating the procedure of handheld device UI and effectively improving the efficiency of the design of Android UI.
