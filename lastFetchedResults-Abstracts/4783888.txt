The knowledge sharing of benchmarking management may urge the choice of enterprise benchmarking, the determination about the benchmarking content and the enterprise innovation. The factors that influence range pole the knowledge sharing between the competitors are the environment recognition ability, organization learning capability, resources deployment ability and imitation-innovation ability, which is a result guidance target. Analyzing the relations about knowledge sharing factor of benchmarking management, establishing the multi-objective optimization model, then optimizing knowledge sharing system, which can provide the support to designate the target of the benchmarking and determinate the benchmarking content.
