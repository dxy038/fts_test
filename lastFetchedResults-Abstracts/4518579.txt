During the past few years, women have begun coming into their own account locally and internationally. "Woman power" even becomes synonymous with power and wealth in Taiwan. As one of the first formal studies on financial institution\Â´s female consumer behavior in Taiwan, the objective of this study is to investigate the key factors used by female consumers and their behavior when making decision in buying financial commodities and services, their perceptions of banking service, and possible female consumer groups classified by their demographic characteristics and lifestyle.
