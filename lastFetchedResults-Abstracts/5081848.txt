Nowadays the majority of higher education establishments in our country aim to graduate highly qualified professionals in all spheres. Heightened attention rides on increased requirements to specialists, which entails continuous improvement of the educational process. Therefore, the objective of development and improvement of education quality assessment technologies in higher educational establishments becomes topical. Application of up-to-date information technologies enables to considerably simplify some procedures related to data collection and processing done to meet the objective, besides, it provides the opportunity to utilize a sophisticated mathematical tool for research. In the framework of the program of quality presentation into the educational process the MG-System computer system was developed. The main purpose of this system is to analyze and process results of expert assessment.
