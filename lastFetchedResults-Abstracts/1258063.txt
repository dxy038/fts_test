Supplier selection process, the most important variable in the effective management of supply chain network is modern. For achieving high quality products and customer satisfaction, an effective supplier selection models and powerful analytical tools for decision support capability of balancing multiple subjective and objective criteria are needed. It is also worth mentioning that most of the manufacturing organizations with more than one criterion to choose encountered of their own supply. In this article, the AHP approach to prioritization of client characteristics and use of quality models in two homes QFD, a new method is proposed to select the best supplier. Finally, to evaluate the effectiveness of the proposed method, the Zam Zam Company has been exemplified.
