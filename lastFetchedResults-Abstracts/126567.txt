The present paper after an introduction focuses on the subjective and objective rules and principals criticism.
The subjective rules and principles of criticism include: mere theoretical study of the author's insight and its application, introducing the inefficiencies and merits, avoiding ambiguity, respecting the priority of the text to criticism, appreciating science and classification in criticism.
The objective rules however consist of: time and place, originality, courtesy and constructiveness, considering the viewpoints of the addressee, coherence, and impartiality.
The conclusion summarizes the main points discussed.
