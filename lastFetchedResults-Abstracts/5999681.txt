Investment income of Innovation projects is uncertainty, the objective existence of uncertainty determines the innovation project investment has certain risk, in this high-risk investment environment to achieve the expected benefits it is necessary to conduct risk analysis of investment projects. Construction of a set of science and innovation investment risk evaluation index system, the application based on information entropy and the combination weight model of AHP and the fuzzy optimization model for a comprehensive assessment respectively, combining the advantages of these three methods, in order to provide a new approach for Innovation project investment risk evaluation and have some examples illustrating the application of this method that it has a good nature, the resulting evaluation results are the same.
