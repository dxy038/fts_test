Much effort has been done to develop more objective quantitative methods to analyze and integrate science and technology information for understanding research trends and research structures. Science &amp; Technology (S&amp;T) text mining is the application of text mining to highly detailed technical material. Co-word analysis is one class of techniques that exploits the use of co-occurrences of items in science and technology text. Bibliometrics and co-word analyses methods were applied on research literature of science and technology of Boeing Co. Its knowledge domains and structures were visualized by network tools.
