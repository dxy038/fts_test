This paper studies each factor which influences IPO pricing in order to make IPO pricing closer to markets, more objective and more reasonable. By analyzing all kinds of factors influencing IPO pricing and applying the multiple linear regression prediction, this paper establishes a rational pricing model of IPO on the stage. With the multiple factor regression equation, this model can quantize the influence on share prices brought by each factor. Finally, by using data analysis method associated with the particular case of China stock markets, the validity of the established model is verified.
