Objective: regarding the role of emotion in health, the present study conducted to determine Relationship between Alexithymia and beliefs about emotion with renal patient’s mental health.
Method: this study was descriptive – correlation. The statistical community was Kermanshah renal patients, that 110 person (54 male & 56 female) were selected by samples available method. To data collection used of GHQ questionnaire, beliefs about emotion and Alexithymia questionnaire. To data analysis used of correlation and regression.
Resalt: the study findings showed that is significant and negative relation between Alexithymia and mental health, but no relationship between beliefs about emotion with renal patient’s mental health.
Conclusion: regarding study findings can be say that in renal patients treatments have to note to role of emotions.
