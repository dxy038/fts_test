The exhaustive research on organization design, its transformation, change management, organizational restructuring for the last two decades makes it a challenging and complex research issue. The central problem is to design the organizations for superior performance on the basis of redesign mechanisms such as uncertainty, differentiation and integration. In the backdrop of the ongoing economic liberalization in India, the specified sector that includes Chemical, Petro-chemical Process Industry, Agro-based Industry, Processed Food Industry, Bio-Chemical related Industry was undertaken for research. The objective is to find the optimum value of redesign mechanisms which can maximize the corporate level performance of the identified groups of the specified sector in the given context.
