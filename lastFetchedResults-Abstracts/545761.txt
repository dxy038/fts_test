Objective
Pfeiffer Syndrome is as rare as Apert syndrome in the Western population. This 
condition is very rare in the Asian population. At the best of our knowledge 
this is the first genetically proven case report from Iran. The authors report with 
a review of literature, the case of a infant with Pfeiffer syndrome, manifested 
by Lacunar skull, ventriculomegaly, bicoronal craniosynostosis,frontal bossing, 
shallow orbits, parrot-like nose, umbilical hernia, broad and medially deviated 
great toes.
