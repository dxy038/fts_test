Enterprise is the main body of Independent Innovation, the nucleus power and dependence of the development of science and technology, and it is also the material base which realizes the scientific and technological progress of our country. Firstly, the thesis makes a generalization of the current independent innovative capacity of enterprises in our country. On the basis of this, according to the principle of the appraisal of innovative capacity, the thesis designs the system of appraisable indexes. Then, taking 14 typical enterprises as the sample, the thesis makes an objective and quantitative analysis of independent innovation by use of the Grey Clustering Analysis method. Finally, the thesis obtains the outcome of gray analysis, and makes an inductive analysis to the appraisable outcome.
