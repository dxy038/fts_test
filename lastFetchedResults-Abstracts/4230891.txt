The evaluation of the tank firepower performance is very important for the demonstration, research, and use of armored vehicles. The Topsis method is applied in this article to comprehensively evaluate the firepower performance of tank, and this method is the effective evaluation method by using the multi-objective decision method to approach the ideal solution, which could effectively solve the difficulty that traditional evaluation methods are hard to integrate multiple evaluation parameters as single-efficiency measurement value, and provide a new approach to comprehensively evaluate the firepower evaluation of tank.
