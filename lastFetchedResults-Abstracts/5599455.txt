With the advent of the digital age, service resources integration of mobile multimedia broadcasting is the objective needs of future development. Firstly, SOA architecture system is introduced on the basis of the overview of China mobile multimedia broadcasting (CMMB). Then, with the concepts of the hierarchical structure model of SOA, CMMB service resources integration model including basic information layer, component layer, service layer, business process layer, surface layer, application layer, enterprise service bus and infrastructure services, is built based on SOA. Finally, Web service technology used to implement the integration model is discussed.
