Quality of life as multidimensional concept and with high importance in life of modern society is influenced by a lot of scientific disciplines including tourism. The objective of this Paper is to investigation tourism development and its impact on different dimension quality of life in rural areas of Kandovan village in Oskoo Township. The method of this research is survey based on use of questionnaire. Target population of this research is inhabitants of kandovan, from which 200 pople sampled through relative stratified sampling. In order to analyze the variables, we applied correlation, regression and nonparametric statistical model. Research results show that level of tourism development is high.
