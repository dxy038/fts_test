The opportunity for profit maximization of thermal stations on the energy market is considered. The change of the size of the station profit depending on the redistribution of fuel consumption between types of generated energy is considered. The choice of this influencing parameter is caused by that, now there is no objective and universal method of fuel costs separation between two kinds of energy. The following approach is offered. It is based on the redistribution of fuel consumption between two types of energy within the framework of the established range.
