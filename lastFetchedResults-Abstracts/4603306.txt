Industrial development model has three basic types and two excessive types,which should be dynamic optimization from low to high based on the development direction of industrial development models,among various types with characteristics of vertical promotion and horizontal coexistence. For China´s civil aircraft,optimization of the development models is a multi-objective programming problem,according to theoritical model and data calculation analysis ,which proved that currently the development model of China´s civil aircraft is investment-oriented, innovation-oriented hybrid,and optimization direction is innovation-oriented.
