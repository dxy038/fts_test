The main objective of this research is to review the performance of young researchers club by using CIPP approach. For this purpose, the population defined with 216 universities' branch which had the research club and were studied accordingly. A questionnaire was used for data gathering that has sent. examines inputs, processes, outputs and background factors. The final result shows that the factors related to inputs club are satisfied in comparison with what expected. By reviewing of the result of background factors we can perceive that currently the main objectives, programs and club's regulations does not clarified well for researchers clubs over the whole country yet and its effectiveness is around 54.7 percent. Moreover there are logical relations between the factors, because there is no significant difference between the performance indices.
