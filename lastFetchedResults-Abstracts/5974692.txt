Meeting agreed customer requirements is a prime objective in this highly competitive business world. Therefore, companies have been trying different ways to eliminate redundant tasks so as to make their operations &#8220;lean&#8221; and &#8220;agile&#8221;. In an agile enterprise, the correct interpretation of and the prompt response to the &#8220;voice of customers&#8221; would require an effective decision making mechanism. This paper explains the design and modelling of a decision support framework which employs a hybrid approach to tackle various operating problems in the manufacturing industry. A case study describing how such a model could be used to handle customer enquiries is quoted to illustrate the concept and characteristics of the proposed system
