The Present Paper is intended to investigate the relationship between the Dome, as a religious symbol in Islam and many other religions , and the prevailing beliefs among nations. It is also the objective of the paper to determine the mythological role of the Dome in the Persian poetry.
Referring to beliefs in Hinduism ,as well as ancient myths of Egypt, symbolism of water and fire, together with certain asian beliefs, specially in southern Russia , attempts will be made to pave the way for a thorough investigation in this regard .
