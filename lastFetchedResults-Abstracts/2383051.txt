The main objective of the present survey has been to investigate the
relation between religious beliefs and principals' management styles.
The research method is in descriptive- measurement. The statistical
collection includes all the principals in three different school levels in
RAMSAR, TONEKABON and ABBAS-ABAD whom they were
totally as 328 people. Among them only 175 people were randomly
picked up in simple way as research sample. The needed information
was gathered through two standard questionnaires: religious beliefs
and management styles. To analyze the data we used Khi-two test.
The results showed that there is a kind of link between religious
beliefs and management styles. Moreover there can be seen sort of
relationship between religious beliefs, management styles (relationcentered)
and principals. Also there is a relationship between religious
beliefs, management styles (relation-centered and duty-centered) and
principals.
