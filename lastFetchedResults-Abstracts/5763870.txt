Activities of the TRON project in these eight years have produced a large amount of technical results which are published as papers, books, and reports. Because such publications of TRON are scattered among diverse journals, proceedings, technical reports, etc., it is difficult for the new TRON researcher and developer to discover relevant documents. The primary objective of this bibliography is to eliminate this difficulty, and to improve the accessibility of technical information of the TRON project. Its contents are restricted to publications on technical issues; non-technical publications are out of its scope
