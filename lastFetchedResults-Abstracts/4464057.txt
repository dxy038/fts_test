This paper firstly gave the definition of equipment manufacturing industries and pointed out that equipment manufacturing industries is of great importance in the development of Anhui economy. On this basis, took industry scale, economic benefits and contribution and technological innovation ability as three major aspects to evaluate the current situation of equipment manufacturing industries in Anhui. And then used Analytic Hierarchy Process and Factor Analysis to evaluate and analyze the current situation of Anhui equipment manufacturing industry according to the data provided by the second national economic census and gave an objective evaluation to it. At last gave some suggestions to the development of Anhui equipment manufacturing industries.
