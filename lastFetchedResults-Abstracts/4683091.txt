As broadcasting and communications convergence is coming, DMB terminals have progressed to network-enabled (CDMA, WiBro, HSDPA, 4G, WLAN, and so on), and more storage and processing capability equipped devices. According to the progress, more rich and user-centric service with communication network and personalized consumption based on metadata and storage are requested . To fill the bill, this paper propose personalized DMB bidirectional contents and metadata server, especially using DMB AF and TV-Anytime metadata.
