The main objective of the paper is to the play Die Physiker of Fredrich Durrenmatt based on textual analysis of Roland Barthes. The analysis is according to codes analyses which are divided into five parts. They include hermeneutic or enigma codes which seek enigmas of the text; proiretic codes which relate to sequences of narrative; symbolic codes which consider binary oppositions; and Codes of semes discuss minimum meanings of the text; using the method, one could analyze the way of dispersing the meanings in the texture of a narrative or text.
