In order to realize the purpose of precise profit management, resolve data isolated phenomena, utilize all kinds of university resources efficiently, and improve the quality and efficiency of university management, a new and feasible URP (university resource planning) project based on digital university is proposed in this paper, which is established based on the ideology of digital, apply the ideas of ERP (enterprise resource planning) and BRP (business resource planning) and combine the fact of university management. An efficient, safe URP management platform by applying some new technologies such as SOA (service-oriented architecture), XML (extensible markup language), Web service and mode integration is set up, which could make leadership scientific decision and management in universities. The objective of communication and management efficiently between teachers and students can be realized by reasonable collocating resources to improve the level, quality and efficiency for universities in China.
