PROMETHEUS is a European research programme which will enhance the technical base for road transport. The objective is to create concepts and solutions which will make traffic perceptibly safer, more economical, with less impact on the environment and will make the traffic system more efficient. In this short presentation of this large programme, the author has selected two sub projects, Pro Car and Pro Net, and he describes tests demonstrations that were performed early in 1989 that illustrate a selection of the concepts which are being studied
