Context and Objective: Knowing common methods of identifying the
victims of human trafficking and contacting them, investigating the physical
features of victims, methods for investigating the crime scene, and the ways
to contact the victims are issues that are under study in this article. In this
respect, the characteristics of the officers dealing with these cases are also
discussed.
Methodology: This research is document-based and library sources have
been used.
Findings: What is emphasized and concluded at the end of this research
is the characteristics of investigation officers with regard to dealing with
the victims of human trafficking and introducing types of victims and
their physical and living features. With the performed investigations, the
identification of criminals and victims of human trafficking is a professional
issue and investigation officers of cases in human trafficking must have the
mentioned characteristics.
Key Words: human trafficking, case investigation officers, features of
human trafficking victims
