Each library software consists of several sections each of which
proportionate to its specific tasks. But, to what extent the available
facilities in library software's conform to the needs of the users, is the
focal point of this paper and its main concentration is on Pars
Azarakhsh library software. The objective is to identify the available
facilities in the circulation module of this software and to suggest the
capabilities for it. In this paper, the criteria for the applicability and
strength of the Pars Azarakhsh library software are considered and
observed in 5 parts such as overdue notice, change in the borrowing
time, reserving the resources, overdue fine as well as settlement and
proposals for improving the software to be suitable and proportionate
to the needs of librarians have been presented.
