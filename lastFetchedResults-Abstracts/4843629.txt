After the careful analysis of governmental policies of Chongqing, this paper tries to develop a new tractable approach of constructing an urban-rural bidirectional logistic system in Chongqing, which is referable for breaking through the dual structure of economy and promoting the urban and the rural to develop evenly and harmoniously. The concrete model of the above urban-rural bidirectional logistic system is given out clearly, and further the Strength, Weakness, Opportunities and Threats are deeply investigated respectively.
