The Present study aims at clarifying the sociological factors affecting the view points of Tehrancitizens toward I.r.I police performance. Variables such as fear of crime and citizen satisfaction were examined. Questionaire was used to gather the required deta from the statistical population which involved citizens above 18 years old.

Findings of the study shows that there is a significant relationship between "mental security" and "objective security", and that respect and accountability are the most important variables affecting citizens' satisfaction.
