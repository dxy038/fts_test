In this article, we studied and analyzed one of fundamental concepts in Bourdiue's theories. For studying of complex and mixed subjects, we analyzed types of capital , social space and field. Bourdiue in capital types emphasizes on complexity and importance of inequality in human society. He also accepts the concept of capital in the theory of Marx and trays to alliance his theory with theory of Weber.Bourdiue Continuously moves in this concept between structure and agency. He presents four types of capital in dimensions of objective ( economic capital) and subjective (Cultural capital, Symbolic capital and social capital) that enhance to inequality system in a social space. For him the capital is a means of distinction in social class so this is difference of Bourdiue's capital theory and other theorists of capital.
