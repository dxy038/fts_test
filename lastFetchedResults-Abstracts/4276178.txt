The ability to attract and retain consumers online is named as the website´s stickiness. From consumers´ perspective, it is described as consumers´ stickiness intention and behavior. A framework is developed to describe how to achieve the objective. In the framework, the factors influencing online consumers´ behavior and corresponding theory basis are considered. Based on this, the forming mechanic of stickiness is analyzed.
