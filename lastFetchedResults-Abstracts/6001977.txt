This paper presents an accessibility system which offers Arabic sign language (ArSL) for the Arabic deaf. This system enables deaf student to access the Web for learning process. In that system the special needs of deaf learners are satisfied, e.g. bilingual information (Arabic text and ArSL), high level of visualization and interactive and explorative learning. The basic objective of our system is the support of the equal rights of deaf people for their access to the educational and training material on the Web.
