EPRI is at the forefront of developing and introducing new technologies to integrate electric utility system operation and planning. This integration has to take place across a wide geographical area for coordinating different planning and operating entities, as well as between system operation and planning within the same entity. Three &#8220;enabling technologies&#8221; are crucial in meeting this objective. They are: the EPRI common information model (CIM) and application program interface (API); the EPRI standards for data communication, e.g. the Inter-Control Center Protocol (ICCP) also known internationally as TASE.2; and EPRI advanced application software and methodologies.
