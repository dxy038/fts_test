Presents some topics about the question relative to actuation of embedded agents in its relationship with the physical environment. Basically the author analyzes the question of uncertainty in embedded agents, presents a design of a fuzzy embedded agent and analyzes the introduction of fuzzy goals to support tasks in embedded agents. These investigations have as the objective analyzing the introduction of fuzzy goals in the GAPPS language for the programming of robots. The author does not present the GAPPS language
