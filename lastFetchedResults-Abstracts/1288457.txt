The main objective of this research was to study the relationship between education and Job vulnerability in Diplomatic Police staff. The research society included all the commanders and officials of diplomatic Police. The sample was 285 diplomatic Police officials who were selected randomly. A questionnaire was used to collect the required data. After gathering and analyzing the data, it was found that there is a meaningful and negative relationship between implementing educational programs and Job vulnerability in Diplomatic Police staff. Accordingly, it is concluded that we can reduce Job vulnerability by implementing educational programs for diplomatic police officials. These findings will be discussed in more detail in this paper.
