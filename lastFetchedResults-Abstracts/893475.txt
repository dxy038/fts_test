In terms of objective, the current study is descriptive and regarding
function, it is an applied research. The article aims to investigate the
relationship between leadership style and organizational climate with
citizenship behavior with regard to the required function of
organizational climate among the employees of general offices at Ardebil
province. Given the broad scatteredness of population, cluster type
sampling method has been used. Volume sample has been estimated
through Kukran 378. After data collection through questionnaire, for
analyzing the research model and confirming its totality and for
analyzing linear relationships between explicit and implicit variables,
structural equations procedure has been used; factor analysis has been
utilized for analyzing hypothetical relations. Also to rank each of the
research variables, Freedmanâ€™s analysis of variance has been used. The
results suggest that there is a signification relationship between
organization climates with organizational citizenship behavior.
