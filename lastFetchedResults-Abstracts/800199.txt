Objective: The present study aims to examine emotional and cognitive schemas in depressed and
non-depressed students. Method: The present study is a retrospectively. The study population
consisted of 208 university students. The participants were selected randomly. To review the
results of the Beck Depression Questionnaire, Young Schema, Leahy Schema of statistical tests
Mann-Whitney U, t-dependent, linear regression, t-independent, Pearson correlation were used
in software SPSS 21.Being interested in the field of study and gender affects depression and
there is a difference between cognitive and emotional schemas in depressed and non-depressed
students and cognitive schemas can predict emotional schemas. Also, emotional and cognitive
schemas can provide a pattern to explain depression.
