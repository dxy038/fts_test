Implementing this systems have a great impact on the performance of that Enterprises.The objective of this paper is investigating the impact of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Systems on the effectiveness of internal controls over financial reporting. The sample include 120 Firm-yaer observations during 2008 to 2012. The hyposis tested using probit regression model.
