By analyzing the content of situation assessment of urban passenger hub, adhering to the principles of objective, practical and scientific, the reasonable evaluation index system was formed. And on this basis, urban passenger transport hub was comprehensively evaluated using AHP and the fuzzy comprehensive evaluation method. Finally in this paper a certain urban passenger transport hub was considered as a case, and the process of application of this method was introduced. Through the case studies, it proves that this method is simple, effective and operable, and the results of evaluation are fair and objective, reasonable and easy.
