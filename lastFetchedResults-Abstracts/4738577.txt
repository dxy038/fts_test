The trend of education informatization exceeds the pace of reform in health quality education. The combination of multi-media technology and website technology is applied into the teaching and practice of health quality. Realizing multi-medizaion, resource globalization, teaching individuality, studying cooperation and education environment virtualization is a development trend of the school´s health quality education. Therefore overall integration of information technology and health quality education should be found to promote the reform of teaching method and achieve the objective of changing the school´s physical education to health quality education.
