This paper briefly summarizes the current development of the electric bicycle industry in our country, and fire hazards were analyzed and evaluated in this dissertation. The fire risk assessment system of electric bicycle was established based on the AHP according to the fire hazard. In this evaluation system, basis data was obtained by the scores of experts. The scores were added up on the multi-objective decision theory of the AHP. The fire safety checklist applicable to the actual situation was build up by the index. The method was used in an instance of a class of electric vehicles proving that this fire risk assessment system to be effective.
