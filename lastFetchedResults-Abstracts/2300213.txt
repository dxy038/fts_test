The significance of meeting the new challenges of urbanization has led to the changes in urban management. Thus, participatory urbanism with an emphasis on community oriented an approach as an efficient planning system which can decrease routine urban problems in the case of investigating carefully with regard to all aspects and the requirements of the residents in that area. The research method is objective and analytical. Data collection was conducted via literature review and documents. Therefore, in order to achieve the objectives of this study, a questionnaire was developed by using Likert scale, and then it was distributed in the neighborhood based on a random sampling method. The obtained data were analyzed via the SPSS software. Finally, the strategies for optimal participation of residents were applied .The results reveals that there is not any significant difference between socioeconomic status and participation of residents. Also, there is a significant relationship between the lack of proper planning and its role in a few citizens in Ali Abad.
