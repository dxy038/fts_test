To secure modern complex system activities, rigid and heavily centralized organization is rather obstacle. To facilitate quick decision, mutual collaboration, and to maintain performability in unexpected severe situation, autonomous decision unit should act at best in intelligent way by itself. Autonomous distributed system concept plays a central role for operating complex systems commonly existing in todays networked society of 21st century. We explain the effectiveness of ADS system paradigm to secure safety critical systems and clarify key issues to realize the objective as intended
