Objective:  The present paper main objective is to evaluate the performance of CIDCA using EFQM model for excellence
Methodology:  A mixed method was used for the survey. Sample was drawn from CIDCA policy makers and senior management.
Findings: Results indicate that based on the said model, CIDCA receives 278.15 out of the possible 1000 score. It is further demonstrated that by taking the necessary steps, it is possible to establish an international library for children and adolescents in Iran. A model for such library is also suggested.
