This article aims the actual situation of China government websites construction in view of user and references on other research result of government websites performance evaluation. We makes use of AHP to evaluate index weights. Based on fuzzy method for quantitive calculation to evaluate comprehensively performance of government website, We designs a reasonable and objective evaluation system of government websites performance. From applicating this method, we can not only measure the general performance level among the district level of government websites but also compare easily among the government website at across and vertical level, owning to arrive at a specific score.
