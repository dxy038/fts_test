The objective of this study is to examine the relationship of earnings management and future returns on assets and future operational cash flows. Reduction of administration, general and sales expenses as well as addition of gains resulting from the sales of long-term assets and overproduction have been utilized in the identification of earnings management.Gunny's Model (2005) was used to test the hypotheses. The statistical population includes producing companies listed on Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE) during 2001 td) 2006. Results indicated that there is no significant relationship between earnings management and future returns on assets and future operational cash flows.
