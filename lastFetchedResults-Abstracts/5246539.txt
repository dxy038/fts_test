This paper presents the main outputs of the Socrates/Minerva project Individualized Learning Enhanced by Virtual Reality IDENTITY 229930-CP-1-2006-1-RO-MINERVA-M. The overall project objective is to produce a high level quality learning environment in an academic European network ensuring an open access to improved educational resources, as well as to the best practices. The main project outputs are: The Enhanced Individualized Learning Environment (EILE) and The VR-Learning Resources Centre, (VR-LRC). 1.
