Knowledge management makes objective planning, policy making, implementing and continual reviewing of knowledge, based on a strategic process. This article introduces balanced scorecard as an overall methodology for KM through reviewing common approaches for knowledge management performance measurement. A balanced scorecard model for knowledge management will be developed to measur the performance of knowledge management strategies. In this model, the required context will be prepared for deploying and measuring the performance of knowledge creating strategies based on four balanced perspective of knowledge including: sympathized knowledge, conceptual knowledge, systemic knowledge and operational knowledge. Afterwards by using a questionnaire and collecting the experts judgments, the proposed model will be verified by Binomial test. Subsequently, the proposed model will be implemented in one of state industrial groups and the results of it will be analyzed. Finally, for measuring the effectiveness of the proposed model in the governmental industrial group, the customer satisfaction data will be analyzed with T-test.
