This paper presents the first results of the OptimaGRID project which focuses on the design of a micro-grid used for industrial area, namely the Tarnos area of the Bayonne harbor. The objective is to size the electrical sources of the micro-grid according with the harbor needs and test its dynamic operation. For these analyses, HOMER<sup>&#174;</sup> and EUROSTAG<sup>&#174;</sup> software have been used.
