It cannot be denied that Personalized Information Service is going to be a vital part of Network service in future. The objective of this paper is to make clear how to provide examinees with personalized specialty recommendation services when they fill in the application form by use of the online registration system. In this paper, the online registration system of adult college entrance examination is used as a platform; and a model of personalized information service for adult college entrance examination online registration system has been obtained through analyzing data during previous years in the adult college entrance examination database and analyzing the latent link between examinees and their specialties which is supported by related expertsÂ´ experiences.
