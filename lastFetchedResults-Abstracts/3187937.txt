This paper applies the Boston matrix to establish an analysis model of Hubei higher educational universities´ development status based on researching 31 regional higher education institutes´ scales, growth rates and R&amp;D input in China. It is first time to put forward the conclusion that Hubei´s higher education still exists in &#8220;question product&#8221;, rather than &#8220;star product&#8221;. Then the paper analyzes competitive advantages &amp; disadvantages, strategic posture of Hubei higher educational institutes; finally proposes strategic approaches which could promote further development of higher education in Hubei province and realize the objective of &#8220;Educational Powerful Province&#8221;.
