College entrepreneurship education carrier can be divided into two types of theoretical teaching and practical operation by the way of teaching. By means of students receiving knowledge of entrepreneurship education, it can be divided into three types of virtual learning and classroom learning and practice. Every vector of entrepreneurship education has its positive side, but the negative side as well. As universities and students individuals, we should have a focus and organic integration when we make the teaching arrangements of entrepreneurship education, because of objective condition. So we can obtain a good effect of entrepreneurship education and learning at the same time.
