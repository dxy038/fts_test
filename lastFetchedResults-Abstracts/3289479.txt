During the past ten years, E-commerce major for undergraduate has been developed rapidly in China. A growing number of companies highlight the urgent needs for employing E-commerce professionals. However, it´s not perfectly matching between professional training and requirements of companies on either knowledge system or practice skills. Hence, how to train specialists for E-commerce is focused by universities and companies. To solve the above problem, in this paper an Engineering-Course Parallel curriculum system for E-commerce characteristic specialty, including training objective, curriculum system, major experiment and practice, and major science research, was designed. By using the new curriculum system we can realize the union of school teaching, students studying and company job, improve students´ major skills and their advantages for job seeking.
