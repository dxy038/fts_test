Date center of each company has embraced more and more business application systems. With the increased requirements of quantity and performance of these systems, the original data center or hosting data center of each company is in face of the potential needs of development. A series of problems of date center should cause more attention. This paper takes the index of evaluation data center as the starting point,through the introduction of the significance and value of the index, can provide certain reference and decision support. In the end, a common data center evaluation which is relatively accurate and objective is put forward.
