The evolution of Information Technology and Communication (ICT) have provided significant changes to modern society. Health is among the areas that has experienced more innovation through ICT constantly searching for processes, methods, equipment and / or devices that may improve service to society. In this scenario, the objective of this work is to develop an application that performs the otoacoustic emissions screening protocol. Thus, OtoLeitor assists the audiologist searching for possible risk factors and economic data partners that may influence, directly or indirectly, the hearing loss of newborn.
