In the boxing, free combat, kickboxing combat events, the attack speed, strength, explosive force, blow impulse etc is an important measured against attack effect and diagnostic technique indicators for players. The general fighting coaches, athletes are very eager to solve the major problem which how to analysis and evaluation the athletes technical of action by effectively obtain these indexes. Develop the system of BJ-2 combat items training and monitoring is based on this demand. This system can formulate scientific training plan, correct guidance and corrective action of athletes for coaches as an objective basis, through the determination of real-time combat athletes finish offensive actions of kinematics and dynamics, technical parameters, etc.
